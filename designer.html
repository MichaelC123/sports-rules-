Expierence needed

At the bare minimum to have an entry level position as an archetectureal designer you need to have you bachelors degree in archetecture. Some key skills that are very usefull are math and science skills, design skills,analytic and problem solving skills ,team building skills, and communication skills

What does a Designer do ?

An archetectual designer is an entry level job at an archetecture firm and what they do is thye make digital plans based on archetectual sketches,visit construction sites, oversee contractors, and facilitate communication with consultants. The average salary is about $43,000 all the way up to about $87,000 per year
